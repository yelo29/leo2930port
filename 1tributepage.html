About José Rizal

José Protacio Rizal Mercado y Alonso Realonda (1861–1896) was a Filipino nationalist, novelist, ophthalmologist, and polymath whose writings inspired the Philippine nationalist movement. He authored key works that criticized colonial rule and argued for reform through education and civic engagement rather than violent revolution.

Rizal travelled extensively, studied medicine and languages, and used his pen to awaken Filipino identity. His legacy remains central to Philippine history and civic education.
